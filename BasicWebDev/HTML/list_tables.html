Grilled Salmon Recipe

Ingredients

  • Slab of Salmon
  • Avocado oil
  • Your favorite seasonings
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Other Spices
      • Garlic
      • Onion
      • Paprika

Cooking Instructions

  1. On a cutting board you can clean easily, slice the salmon slab into 8ox filets
  2. Preheat grill to 400 degrees F.
  3. Lightly drizzle oil onto the salmon
  4. Add your favorite seasonings
  5. Grill for 12 minutes and flip after 8 minutes
